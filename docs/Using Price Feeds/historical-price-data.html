However, `AggregatorV3Interface` also exposes functions which can be used to retrieve price data of a previous round ID. > ðŸ“˜ > > The [Decentralized Data Model](../architecture-decentralized-model) describes how Price Feeds are aggregated from many data sources and published on-chain. # Historical Rounds Price Feeds are updated in rounds. Rounds are identified by their `roundId`, which increases with each new round (please note that the increase may not be monotonic). Knowing the `roundId` of a previous round allows contracts to consume historical price data. > ðŸš§ > > Important: if you are deriving a round id without having observed it before, the round may or may not be complete. To check if it is, you must validate that the timestamp on that round is not 0. ## Solidity
